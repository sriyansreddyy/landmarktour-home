This is everyone of us at Gents Seat, Yercaud on 28th December, 2022.

We’re class 10 students of St. Joseph’s School, Malakpet, Hyderabad. Our school organized a 5-day trip to Yercaud, a hill-station in Tamil Nadu, 1623m(5325ft) above sea level, from 26th December to 30th December 2022. We were a group of 98 students and 9 teachers. The whole trip cost us 15000 INR per person. This tour was an emotional roller coaster ride for us. Finally, we reached home by catching a running train. We were just speechless with happiness on seeing our parents again after 5 long exhausting days and many of us fell ill, not even half of us attended school 2 days later...
